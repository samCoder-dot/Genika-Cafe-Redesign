Our Story

A gathering place
unlike any other

Located just steps from the Walter E. Washington Convention Center, Genika Café was born from a belief that great coffee and great company belong together. We source beans from some of the world's most respected growing regions — each chosen for its unique flavour profile and roasted to honour its natural character.


As the sun sets, we transform into an intimate tavern where friends gather over handcrafted cocktails, local wines, and the kind of conversation that lingers long after last call.
